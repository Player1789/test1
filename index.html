SUSHI

uramaki

A Japanese food in the form of a roll made with rice on the outside and filling on the inside

nigiri

A small oval block of cold rice topped with wasabi and a thin slice of fish, prawn, etc, and sometimes held together by a thin band of seaweed

inari

Inari Sushi (or inarizushi) is essentially a type of sushi consisting of sushi rice (rice lightly seasoned with a mixture of vinegar and sugar) stuffed in fried tofu skin.

maki

A small segment cut from a long roll of cold rice and various other ingredients wrapped in a sheet of seaweed

MEAL

bento

A single-portion take-out meal. Bento is served in a lacquered decorated box that is divided into sections for holding individual portions of food.

donburi

Japanese rice bowl dish. Fish, meat, vegetables or other ingredients simmered together and served over rice. Donburi meals are served in oversized rice bowls also called donburi.

curry

You can try delicious Japanese curry at Hikari. Curry with katsu is the best combination for people who want a solid meal.

noodles

We have 2 different kinds of noodles, udon and ramen. Udon is a type of thick wheat flour noodle and Ramen is using thinner wheat noodles. Hikari offers a wide variety of noodle dishes with two kinds of noodles.
